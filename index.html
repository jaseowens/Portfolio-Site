About Me

I am currently a student at the University of Alabama at Birmingham where I am studying Computer Science and Graphic Design. I currently work for UAB as a web developer for an inter-departmental web application that is used by over 500 individuals on a day to day basis. I have been in charge of redesigning the whole site as well as improving functionality in a variety of places for the past year. In the graphic design world I was previously employed at UAB where I made posters, flyers and announcements for the School of Optometry that were displayed across campus.
